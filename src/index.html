ABOUT US

Arrange a dental check-up for all the family at our friendly practice in Brooklyn. Here at Family Dental Practice, we provide a variety of dental treatments for both NHS and private patients. Our proficient dentists have more than 100 years of combined experience which they utilise when undertaking all procedures. This wealth of experience ensures that we have the knowledge and the capability to make even the most nervous of patients feel at ease and comfortable . Tailoring all treatments to your needs, we carry out everything from general check-ups to cosmetic dentistry, including veneers and teeth whitening. Unlike other practices, our surgery is family run and this enables us to keep patients at the heart of our service. Transform your smile and feel more confident. Book your appointment today!

NHS TREATMENTS

Maintain excellent oral health with the variety of dental treatments we provide for NHS Patients. From dental check-ups to carrying out root canal services and tooth removals, our expert dentists proficiently carry out your treatment.

PRIVATE TREATMENTS

Rejuvenate your skin and achieve a smoother and radiant complexion with the facial aesthetics we provide. From age-defying derma fillers to revitalising chemical peels, we have the treatment to achieve your desired look.
